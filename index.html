About Us

At La Couture Beauty Parlour, we believe in beauty with a conscience. We have created a salon that offers the highest quality hair services in a setting that is healthier for the environment, our guests and our staff. We carry high quality professional hair products designed to ensure our guests look their best, both in the salon and at home. The hair care products we carry have been carefully chosen based both on performance and eco-sensitivity. Our design and philosophy work hand in hand. We are committed to providing healthy hair care with a low impact on the environment.
